About Us

Authentic Prophetic Guidance, Accessible to Every Heart

Timeless Hadith brings the verified words and teachings of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) into one searchable, beautifully organized resource. No paywalls. No distractions. Just authentic guidance, clearly presented.

Our Mission, Vision, and Values

Mission

To make authentic Prophetic traditions accessible, searchable, and beautifully presented for every Muslim seeking guidance in their daily life and spiritual journey.

Vision

A world where the Sunnah of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) is just a search away, free from barriers, presented with reverence and care, and trusted by Muslims everywhere.

Values

We are committed to authenticity — every hadith is verified against the six major canonical collections. We are committed to accessibility — no paywalls, no subscriptions, no ads. And we are committed to respect — the Prophet's guidance deserves a platform built with integrity and thoughtfulness.

Why Timeless Hadith?

Verified from Canonical Sources

Completely Free and Private

Our Sources: The Kutub al-Sittah

Every hadith featured on Timeless Hadith comes from the six major authenticated collections (Kutub al-Sittah), compiled by renowned scholars and accepted across all schools of Islamic jurisprudence (madhabs). These collections form the foundation of Sunnah studies.

Sahih al-Bukhari Imam Muhammad al-Bukhari
Sahih Muslim Imam Muslim ibn al-Hajjaj
Sunan an-Nasa'i Imam Ahmad an-Nasa'i
Sunan Abi Dawud Imam Abu Dawud al-Sijistani
Jami at-Tirmidhi Imam Muhammad at-Tirmidhi
Sunan Ibn Majah Imam Muhammad Ibn Majah

For deeper verification and cross-referencing, sunnah.com is an invaluable resource. We always recommend studying hadiths with qualified Islamic scholars to understand their context and application in your life.

Our Approach

Timeless Hadith is built with respect for your privacy and speed. The site uses no external tracking scripts, no advertising networks beyond what is necessary, and minimal dependencies. Your data stays on your device—we don't collect or sell anything about your browsing.

We comply with relevant data protection standards, including Saudi Arabia's Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL). For complete transparency, please see our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

This project is open source. If you spot an error, have a suggestion, or want to contribute improvements, visit our GitHub repository.
