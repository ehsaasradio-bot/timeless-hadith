Our Story

Making the Prophetic tradition
accessible to every heart

Timeless Hadith was built with a single purpose: to bring the authentic words of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ closer to anyone who seeks them — clearly, beautifully, and without barriers.

Our Mission

The hadiths of the Prophet ﷺ contain timeless wisdom for every aspect of life — from how we treat our neighbours to how we approach our Lord. Yet for many Muslims and curious minds, this treasure remains scattered across hundreds of volumes, hard to navigate and harder to search.

We set out to change that. Timeless Hadith brings together authentic narrations, organised by theme, in a clean and accessible format — so that the guidance of the Prophet ﷺ is always just a search away.

Our Sources

All hadiths are sourced from the six major authenticated hadith collections (the Kutub al-Sittah), widely accepted by Muslim scholars across all schools of thought.

Sahih al-Bukhari Imam al-Bukhari
Sahih Muslim Imam Muslim
Sunan an-Nasa'i Imam an-Nasa'i
Sunan Abi Dawud Imam Abu Dawud
Jami' at-Tirmidhi Imam at-Tirmidhi
Sunan Ibn Majah Imam Ibn Majah

We always recommend verifying hadiths through qualified Islamic scholars. The online database sunnah.com is an excellent cross-reference.

Our Approach

Timeless Hadith is built entirely without external dependencies — no tracking scripts, no advertising networks, no external fonts or CDNs. Everything loads from our own servers, keeping your browsing private and the page fast.

We comply with Saudi Arabia's Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) and apply industry-standard security headers across the site. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

The site is open source. You are welcome to view the code on GitHub, report issues, or contribute improvements.
