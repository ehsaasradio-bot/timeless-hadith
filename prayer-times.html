Hadith on Zakat
إِنَّ اللَّهَ فَرَضَ عَلَيْهِمْ صَدَقَةً تُؤْخَذُ مِنْ أَغْنِيَائِهِمْ وَتُرَدُّ عَلَى فُقَرَائِهِمْ
"Indeed, Allah has made Zakat obligatory upon them. It is to be taken from their wealthy and returned to their poor."
Sahih al-Bukhari 1395 · Narrated by Ibn Abbas (RA)
Quranic Verse
وَأَقِيمُوا الصَّلَاةَ وَآتُوا الزَّكَاةَ وَمَا تُقَدِّمُوا لِأَنفُسِكُم مِّنْ خَيْرٍ تَجِدُوهُ عِندَ اللَّهِ
"And establish prayer and give Zakat, and whatever good you put forward for yourselves — you will find it with Allah."
Surah Al-Baqarah · Quran 2:110

Important Notice

The figures presented by this calculator are intended solely as an indicative estimate to assist in your initial Zakat planning. They are based on live spot prices for gold and silver and widely accepted nisab thresholds (85g gold / 595g silver), in accordance with the scholarly consensus of the four major madhabs.

This tool does not constitute a fatwa, a formal Zakat assessment, or a substitute for qualified Islamic financial advice. The obligation of Zakat carries significant religious and legal weight, and its precise calculation — particularly with respect to the valuation of mixed assets, business inventory, agricultural produce, livestock, and outstanding debts — must be performed in strict accordance with established Islamic jurisprudence and under the guidance of a qualified scholar or certified Islamic finance authority.

Gold and silver spot prices are retrieved daily and are approximate. Currency conversion rates are indicative only. Timeless Hadith accepts no liability for reliance upon these figures.
