1 Executive Summary

Timeless Hadith is a well-engineered Islamic reference platform serving 7,277 hadiths from Sahih al-Bukhari via a static HTML/CSS/JS frontend connected to Supabase PostgreSQL, deployed on Cloudflare Pages. The site demonstrates enterprise-level security headers (A grade), strong accessibility fundamentals (WCAG 2.2 AA mostly compliant), and a clean Apple-inspired design. However, this fresh live audit reveals several issues that were either introduced after the last audit or previously under-reported.

Biggest Risks (Requires Immediate Attention)

#RiskImpact
1blog.html and prayer-times.html serve NO meta tags to crawlers — live WebFetch confirmed title, description, OG, Twitter, and JSON-LD are all absent from the server responseThese 2 high-value pages are invisible to Google, social platforms, and AI search engines
2category.html relies on client-side JS for canonical, OG, and title — Googlebot may not execute JS before indexing97 category pages (the bulk of indexable content) may have incorrect search snippets
32.1 MB of disk waste — duplicate /website/ directory (1.4 MB), unused /archive/ (476 KB), /TH Logo/ (231 KB)Bloated repository; potential accidental exposure of old files
4Unused CairoPlay fonts still precached in service worker (80.6 KB)Every visitor downloads 80 KB of unused font data
5Google Client ID is a placeholder — OAuth login is non-functionalUsers cannot sign in; bookmarks are device-locked

Quick Wins (Under 1 Hour Total)

1. Add static meta tags to blog.html and prayer-times.html head sections (20 min). 2. Delete unused CairoPlay font files + remove from sw.js (5 min). 3. Add WebP/JPG/WOFF2 cache rules to _headers (5 min). 4. Expand 6 short title tags to 50-60 characters (15 min). 5. Add Twitter card tags to categories.html and category.html (10 min). 6. Delete /website/, /archive/, /TH Logo/ directories (2 min).

Revenue / Growth Opportunities

Blog posts with proper BlogPosting schema can appear in Google Discover (billions of monthly impressions). The prayer-times tool with WebApplication schema can rank for "prayer times" queries (high-volume Islamic keyword). Category pages with ItemList schema show rich snippets with hadith counts. Urdu translation would double the addressable audience.

Trust Issues

No cookie consent banner despite using Cloudflare Analytics. No social proof or testimonials on homepage. Google OAuth non-functional (placeholder client ID). Author/expertise signals missing from blog content. Admin panel discoverable at /admin.html without IP restriction.
