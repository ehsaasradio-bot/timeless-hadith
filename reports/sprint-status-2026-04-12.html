    ⚠ Things to Know Before Pushing

  • Worker redeploy needed separately — worker/src/index.js was changed but Cloudflare Workers deploy independently via cd worker && npx wrangler deploy. The Pages deploy will NOT automatically update the Worker.
  • Rate limiter is in-memory — resets on every cold start. For production-scale limiting, upgrade to Cloudflare KV or Durable Objects in a future sprint.
  • Stale service worker on existing visitors — sw.js version is bumped to th-v8. Returning visitors will get the old cached version until their browser checks for SW updates (usually within 24 hours or on next visit).
  • backups/ folder — intentionally untracked (excluded by .gitignore). Do not add it to git.
